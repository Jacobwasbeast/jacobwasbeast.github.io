Minecraft Forge Auto Mod Downloader

Similar to the Minecraft Auto Mod Downloader, but specifically designed to work with Minecraft Forge mods. It utilizes SFTP for mod downloads.

Features:

  • Automated mod downloading and installation for Minecraft Forge.

  • Supports SFTP for secure and efficient mod retrieval.

  • Streamlines the process of managing Forge mods.

Installation:

  1. Download the mod from here .

  2. Extract the contents to a preferred location on your system.

  3. Configure SFTP settings to connect to your mod repository.

  4. Run the mod and enjoy hassle-free downloading of Forge mods for perfectly synced client!
