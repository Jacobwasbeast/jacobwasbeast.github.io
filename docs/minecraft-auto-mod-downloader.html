Minecraft Auto Mod Downloader

A tool designed to automate the process of downloading and installing mods for Minecraft. It utilizes FTP for mod downloads.

Features:

  • Automates mod downloading and installation process.

  • Supports FTP for efficient mod retrieval.

  • Simplifies the management of Minecraft mods.

Installation:

  1. Download the tool from here .

  2. Extract the contents to a desired location on your system.

  3. Configure FTP settings as necessary.

  4. Run the mod and enjoy hassle-free downloading of mods for a perfectly synced client!
