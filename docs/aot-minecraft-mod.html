AOT Minecraft Mod

An Attack on Titan-themed Minecraft modification, bringing elements from the popular anime series into the game.

Features:

  • Titans roaming the Minecraft world.

  • ODM Gear for players to traverse terrain.

  • Custom AOT-themed items and structures.

Installation:

  1. Download the mod file from here .

  2. Place the downloaded file into your Minecraft mods folder.

  3. Launch Minecraft and explore the world of Attack on Titan!
