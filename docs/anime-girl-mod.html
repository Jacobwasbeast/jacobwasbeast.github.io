Anime Girl Minecraft Mod

A mod that introduces anime girl characters into the Minecraft world, offering a new aesthetic and experience for players.

Features:

  • Adds various anime girl character models.

  • Custom animations and interactions.

  • Enhances visual aesthetics of the game.

Installation:

  1. Download the mod file from here .

  2. Place the downloaded file into your Minecraft mods folder.

  3. Launch Minecraft and immerse yourself in the world of anime girls!
